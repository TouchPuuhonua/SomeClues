Aberrant Epistemology, or Cult Science

Scholars in all fields do research, publish books and papers, attend conferences and criticise (often very aggressively) the validity of their colleagues' work.

What is not obvious is the nature of the methodologies and other structures of integrity which allow some fields to genuinely and ongoing extend human knowledge while other fields are merely going through the motions.

A number of fields of study are cults: closed systems of thought actively hostile to real research data, merely recirculating superstitions and playing social and intellectual dominance games.

Some of these abberrant scholastic fields address fundamental concerns to human beings. These seem to include

  • The study of education
  • Penology
  • Psychotherapy
  • Most sociology and anthropology

Most other fields contain pockets of cult science which significantly impede the progress of the field as a whole.

The tendency of society as a whole to trust the findings of researchers without checking the integrity of their research programs contributes significantly to the failure of much of our social, economic and political systems.

Society generously funds credentialed researchers operating within closed systems of thought and collaborates with these pseudo-scholars by ignoring or punishing researchers who attempt to perform valid research in their field.
