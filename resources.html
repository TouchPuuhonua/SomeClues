Practical Training and Experiences

The word training is used both for imparting the lowest level mindless skills through rote drill and conditioning, and for the highest, most creative intensive educational experiences. Training in the latter sense refers to all of the aspects of education that go beyond the transfer of information and intellectual understanding.

Artists, musicians and scientists talk about where they got their training and revere those institutions which reliably impart the discernment and discipline required to do excellent work while nurturing creativity and growing the student's intuition.

Many organizations offer training programs for individuals which claim to train or coach or enlighten those individuals in a way which will give them an extrardinarily great life. Most of those organizations have a belief system which members must ascribe to. Many have questionable grounding in reality. Some are cults. A few offer empowering training going well beyond what is commonly believed possible with a minimum of dogma.

It is particularly useful to get training from two or more of these organizations, as the effect is synergistic and helps compensate for the tendency of most powerful organizations to create an inbred culture.

Landmark Education Corporation

Landmark Education Corporation is the heir to the once-controversial transformational organization called est (the latin verb to be). When the inventor of est, Werner Erhard, realized that a more straightforward approach to transformation might be available he retired the est training and created the ancestor of today's Landmark Forum workshop. He later sold the organization to the staff who now own it cooperatively and run it through a creative consensus process which they teach as part of their strategic design technology.

Landmark has designed a core curriculum which is a fast and effective training for obtaining access to transformation in one's life. They also has an excellent communication program and a special post-graduate curriculum called Wisdom.

If Landmark has a biggest flaw, it is that they are not always gentle about promoting their programs and encouraging participants in their programs to spread the word. A high degree of enthusiasm often comes across as false or manipulative. We suggest ignoring any proselitization and instead sharing with graduates we trust.

The Human Awareness Institute

The Human Awareness Institute, usually abbreviated HAI and pronounced like hi offers the Love, Intimacy and Sexuality workshop series. These extraordinary and lovely workshops gently teach the skills of love and intimacy - not sex - we're talking about the real, warm, nurturing, connecting unconditional love and joyous intimacy which most people are starving for (which is part of why they don't get it).

Each workshop is one weekend long at a retreat center and consists of experiential exercises where the participant gets to learn their own lessons. HAI has almost no dogma. Do not confuse these workshops with sex workshops. Yes, there is a wonderful impact on one's sexuality and sex life, and that is not the main focus of the workshops. Highly (HAI-ly?) recommended.

Compassionate Communication

The Center for Nonviolent Communication, founded by Marshall Rosenberg, teaches communication skills which allow people to honestly express their needs and emotions without laying anything on the person they are speaking with. They teach an extraordinary sensitivity to the maniulative and coercive use of language and offer an antidote. The Center is very involved with peacework throughout the world, yet these are very useful skills for anyone to have and are particularly useful in relationship and partnership.

Body Electric

O.K., all that stuff above sounds really great, but what about sex? I'm glad you asked! There are lots of programs which teach how to have mind-blowing sex. The most common programs are based on Tantra, which is the East Indian yogic approach to sexuality. Another approach is Quidoshka, which is based on North American Indian, particularly Navaho and Cherokee traditions. These are great and they come along with a certain amount of the traditional worldview of those traditions. Body Electric is different.

Body Electric comes out of an eclectic tradition including massage, Chinese Taoist practice and Queer sexuality - that's Queer as in homosexual. Body Electric is a school of Massage which also teaches Erotic Massage and Sacred Spirituality. It was originally founded by Joseph Kramer and was only for men, mostly gay or bisexual men. Annie Sprinkle (yes, that Annie Sprinkle) and some other wild women generalized the practices to include women's sexuality - all kinds of women's sexuality.

Under the various directors Body Electric has offered workshops for working with male/female sexuality, same-gender sexuality and accesses to the erotic which transcend any conventional category. Many people find their unique approach both powerful and refreshingly modern. The variety of programs has expanded and contracted over the years so check the current catalog.

Burning Man

Burning Man is hard to describe - no, it's impossible to describe, but let's say a few things; after all, this website is all about clues, we don't have to trap wild things and put them in boxes. Burning Man is an experiment in radical expression - at the individual, group and community levels. It is a place to have our conventional ideas blown open. It is a place to fire our creativity. It is the best party on the planet.

Burning Man is a festival (what might that word mean?) which takes place each year at the end of August in a dry lake bed in the middle of a desert in Nevada. Approximately 50,000 people come and build a city together which exists for only one week and is then completely erased from the desert, but participant's lives are permanently marked by the experience.

Check out the website. Attend their events in your local region and meet local Burners. The event sells out so apply for tickets early.
