The Facts of Life

Not everyone who makes lots of money pays taxes.

Aging and death are not certain and both may well be reversible.

The erotic is bigger than sexuality and human sexuality is usually only indirectly connected with reproduction.

We die a bit in each moment. When we try to hold onto who we are we become more dead. It is when we voluntarily give up who we are in each moment in favor of new being that we are most alive.

The whole Universe is a free gift. Our life is a free gift. Receiving life and the Universe as a gift, appreciating them as a gift, grants the receiver great power.
