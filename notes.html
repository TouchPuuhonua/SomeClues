Notes to use

If I don't go to the Doctor, I'm not really sick.

Dear Company X: I'll offer you this deal: I'll try to break into your computer system; If I fail, I'll let you know you've got pretty good security and you owe me nothing; if I succeed, I'll give you a report of how you're vulnerable and help you tighten your security - all according to my published fee schedule: Shall we proceed? -- Most companies won't go for this, even if the offerer is a prestigious security firm; the delusion is the "If I don't go to the Doctor, I'm not really sick." fallacy.
